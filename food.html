Discover the Link Between Food and Mental Health

Hover over each of the following pictures to find out more:

Sugary Foods

Evidence shows that these foods affect memory, attention and increase the vulnerability to depression and anxiety

Brown rice, pasta, bread, nuts and seeds

These foods release energy more slowly to prevent you feeling tired, irritable or depressed

Drinking fluids

Water, milk and juices keep you hydrated so that you can concentrate and think clearly

Protein-rich foods

Protein contains chemicals called amino acids which your brain needs to help regulate your thoughts and feelings

Fruits and Vegetables

Eating fruit and vegetables supports healthy digestion and should be increased when you are feeling anxious to avoid constipation

Alcohol

Drinking too much alcohol can disrupt chemicals in the brain that are important for good mental health
